Homeless Sees Backpack - Finds Bombs

Tuesday, September 20, 9:00 P.M.

"oh noes"

Weird, huh? Bomb stories left and right! Lee Parker finds a backpack. What would you do? If you're affluent or at least have a house, you probably wouldn't take it. Lee Parker is different. He's homeless and carries his stuff in a blue doggy bag. He needs a backpack. Auspiciously, he finds a backpack. He peeks inside and is shocked. Cluttered wires. It was five bombs. 911 is called, and he is hailed a hero. Parker said, "He was doing the right thing." I don't see that view. He was just taking a random backpack of the street. That's not right. This action doesn't make him any more of a hero than a thief. However, he got lucky this time, and it was a bomb. He stills needs a backpack, though.
