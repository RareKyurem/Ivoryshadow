Schools and City Bus Collides; Kills Six

Tuesday, November 1, 8:35 P.M

"crash"

This morning, a school bus rear-ended a Ford Mustang and then bounced off a pillar into the way of a city bus.

The school bus had no children inside, but both drivers of the buses died. The other four killed were on the city bus, but ten other people were injured, an aide from the school bus, and nine from the city bus. Two were in critical condition.

Many of the workers on the city bus were laborers heading to a suburb.

Investigators have recovered recordings, but they could be damaged.
