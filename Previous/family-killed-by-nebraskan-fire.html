The Speer family of six in Nehawka, Nebraska died in a house fire yesterday morning.

The dad is Mike, the mom is Michelle, and their four daughters are Ellie, Addilyn, Emma, and Anniston.

Firefighters arrived, but they couldn't go inside to stop the fire.

They only thing they could do is fight the fire from outside. The firefighters were working 12-hour shifts, but the people inside were dead already.

The community has been very encouraging as the fire crews investigate the cause of the fire.
