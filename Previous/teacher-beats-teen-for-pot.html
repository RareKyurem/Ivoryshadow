A 48-year old science teacher in a Bronx charter school was arrested today for beating up one of his students. Why? For pot, of course.

The teacher, Kevin Pope, asked the teen for a pound of marijuana, giving $4,000 to the kid. However, the teen didn't give the weed, and Pope asked more the money back. When the teen didn't give the cash, he beat the kid - really hard. He stormed away, taking the teen's wallet, iPhone, and gold chain. The teen arrived at the hospital battered and bruised, with several deep cuts to the face, which need staples to close.

According to several friends of Pope, he was a good-natured man, helpful, and kind.

He was charged with assault and robbery.
