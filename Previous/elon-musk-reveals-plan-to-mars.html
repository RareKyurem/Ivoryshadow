Elon Musk Reveals Plan to Mars

Tuesday, September 27, 6:00 P.M.

"yay"

Subscribe to the Ivoryshadow Daily Newsletter

Today, in an eagerly awaited presentation, Elon Musk revealed his plan to colonize Mars, which includes using reusable rockets to move thousands of people over to Mars, with the hope that these people can build a self-sustaining colony. The rockets will have a ton of thrust, 27 million to be exact. SpaceX is launching an spacecraft to Mars in 2018. Prices will be steep, but not as much - only $200,000. The spacecraft will be especially plush, considering the 3-4 month trip. It will be loaded with a sorts of entertainment, from movies to games.

I think this is a big step in our plan to colonize other planets. However, I think that more research should directed toward preserving our planet, rather than leaving our planet behind so we don't have to deal with it. Mars could be a getaway location if we every are in trouble though.
