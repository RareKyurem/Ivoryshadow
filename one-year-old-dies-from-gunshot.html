One Year Old Dies From Gunshot

Sunday, October 2, 5:00 P.M.

"rocks n stuff"

Wow, it's another news story involving some form of violence of death. This Saturday, a one-year-old boy with the last name McDonough, died from a gun wound. An adult left a gun, a .45 caliber, unattended and one of the two brothers in the family shot the gun. Adults were present in the same room, but they did not see the accident occur. Investigators don't really know what happened, whether the one-year-old shot himself or the three-year-old shot the younger brother.
