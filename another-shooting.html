Another Shooting

Wednesday, September 28, 9:40 P.M.

"pew pew"

Jeez, it feels like that we've been having a lot of shootings this past year. Here's another one, but at least there something kind of comforting. No one died. Yay...

This one happened in South Carolina. A teenager with the last name of Osborne opened fire on a nearby elementary school, wounding two students and one teacher. Wait, someone did die. At 1:44 P.M., the teenager called his grandparents in tears, and when his grandparents came over, they found his father, Jeffrey Osborne, dead from bullets. At 1:45 P.M., a shooting was reported at the school. One student was injured from a bullet to the leg; another was shot in the foot, and the teacher was shot in the shoulder.

School was canceled for the rest of the week. Yay, no school!
