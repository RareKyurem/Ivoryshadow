Wednesday, October 19, 8:50 P.M.

This morning, a bagel shop by the name of Portland Bagelworks was destroyed by an explosion, leading to 8 injuries and no deaths.

No one died! Rejoice!

Three firefighters, two police officers, and civilians were injured in the explosion at around 10 A.M.

The most probable reason for the explosion was a gas leak, though people were already told to move away from starting at 9:10. No wonder no one died.
