3 Year Old Shoots Himself

Thursday, October 13, 6:00 A.M.

"ooh whats this! its a gun"

Hey! A little boy shot himself again. The event occurred this morning, in Waukegan, Illinois. Officials detail the little boy waking up in the wee hours are firing the handgun on his father's dresser. The father owned an official handgun owner's license, but I guess he forgot to lock in up this time. The 9 mm Diamondback was the gun the fatally ended that little boy's life. The parents seem very distraught, which leads convinces investigators that it was a mistake.

The parents weren't charged with anything because it appeared to be a mistake, but I think otherwise. They were still indirectly responsible for the accident for not locking up the gun, so they should be charged with involuntary manslaughter. This sort of accident has occurred quite frequently; we should be more cautious.

Don't leave those guns out kids.
